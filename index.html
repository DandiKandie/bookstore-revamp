Welcome to Book Corner Tampa Bay

An Out of Print Hardcover Book Store in Brandon, FL

What We Offer

The Spirit of our business is bringing people and books together. Whether it is a paperback written by your favorite author, or a collectible item whose spirit moves you! We have what you need. To have you walk out thrilled with what you find, drives us to deliver you the best experience a used book store can provide.

We offer our services to the great community of Brandon/Tampa, FL area with a shop open to the public for over 27 years. We claim the title, for one of the longest running used book stores that deal with both hardcover and paperback books in the Brandon/Tampa area!

We carry collectibles and rare books, more than half of our inventory is of the gently used variety. Our mission to you, is to provide a consistent price, we sell our gently used books for half of the original cover price. That is our price every day, all day.

What We Specialize In

We are a lover of books! We proudly call ourselves Book-o-Holics. It is more than just a business, it is an addiction we share with our customers.

Novels: We carry Science Fiction, Horror, Fantasy, Mysteries, Thrillers, Teen Fiction and Manga in both hardcover and paperback. Non-Fiction: We have both hardcover and paperback books in all genres of this category; from Cookbooks to Zoology. We have something for everyone!

Our book buyer: Michael, is available to you typically Thursday to Saturday. Please always call ahead to confirm he is working, if you plan on selling your books. He is the only buyer, all other staff will not be able to assist in the purchasing of books, so please call ahead of time to make sure he is in the office.
